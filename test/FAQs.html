Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your tour packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, transport, guided tours, and some meals. Details vary by package.

Do you offer customized trips?

Yes! We can tailor trips based on your interests, schedule, and budget.

How can I book an Umrah package?

You can book directly through our website or contact our support team for assistance with the booking process.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 14 days before departure are fully refundable. After that period, partial refunds or credit options may apply.

Do you provide airport pickup and drop-off?

Yes, airport transfers are included in most of our packages. You can also request them as an add-on service.

Are your tours family-friendly?

Absolutely. We offer family-oriented packages that include safe and fun activities suitable for children.

What should I pack for an Umrah trip?

We recommend modest and comfortable clothing, prayer essentials, personal toiletries, and valid travel documents. A detailed checklist will be shared after booking.

Do I need a visa for Saudi Arabia?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. For Umrah, we assist with the visa application process. For tourism, e-visas are available for many countries.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the Contact page, WhatsApp, or email. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you.
